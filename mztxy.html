Detective Academy

"Detective Academy" is a spin-off of the popular Chinese variety show "Who's the Murderer". This reality show features a group of intelligent and quick-witted contestants who take on various reasoning challenges, logic-based games, and team-based competitions. Combining mystery, deduction, and humor, the show tests participants' problem-solving abilities while entertaining the audience with their unique personalities and interactions.

The series has aired for eight seasons:

  • Detective Academy S1

    • In "Detective Academy Season 1", Mingming Shao serves as the teaching assistant, while the trainees include Eazin Pu, Jojo Tang, Cheese Qi, Jacky Zhou, Stefan Guo, Sky Shi, Youcheng Pan, Chunyun Wang, and Dongzhe Lang.

    Throughout the season, contestants advance on a game board by rolling dice, engaging in high-level intelligence and reasoning challenges. The first trainee to reach the final space and win the competition in each episode earns the role of "Detective Assistant" for that week’s case in "Who's the Murderer Season 5"

  • Detective Academy S2

    • In "Detective Academy Season 2", the trainees include Eazin Pu, Jojo Tang, Cheese Qi, Jacky Zhou, Stefan Guo, Sky Shi, and Mingming Shao.

    The season is divided into two semesters. In the first semester, contestants participate in immersive reasoning-based "scripted murder mystery" games. In the second semester, they spend nine days together in a fun and laughter-filled cohabitation experience, engaging in intense intellectual battles.

  • Detective Academy S3

    • In "Detective Academy Season 3", Mingming Shao serves as the teaching assistant, while the trainees include Eazin Pu, Stefan Guo, Cheese Qi, Jojo Tang, Sky Shi, Vincent Cao, Yancy He, and Dr. Ye (Huoshu).

    Throughout the season, contestants engage in competitive games during four "two-day, one-night" cohabitation sessions, sharing unexpected and joyful moments in the academy. The overall winner of the season earns the role of host for "Detective Club".

  • Detective Academy S4

    • In “Detective Academy Season 4”, Rosalyn Luo serves as the investigator, with Mingming Shao as the teaching assistant. The trainees include Eazin Pu, Stefan Guo, Cheese Qi, Sky Shi, Vincent Cao, and Yancy He.

    This season takes place in a mysterious village, where contestants use clues and reasoning to search for a hidden treasure. Over three “three-day, two-night” sessions, the participants engage in both intellectual and physical challenges while sharing a warm and entertaining cohabitation experience.

  • Detective Academy S5

    • In "Detective Academy Season 5", Xin Wu takes on the role of the housekeeper, while Mingming Shao serves as the teaching assistant. The trainees include Eazin Pu, Stefan Guo, Cheese Qi, Sky Shi, Di Yang, Bo Pang, Jojo Tang, Vincent Cao, and Yancy He.

    This season, the contestants move into a new mysterious village, where they embark on six unprecedented and imaginative exploration journeys, each based on a thrilling and unpredictable storyline.

  • Detective Academy S6

    • In "Detective Academy Season 6", the trainees include Eazin Pu, Stefan Guo, Jacky Zhou, Cheese Qi,Vincent Cao, Yancy He, Jojo Tang, Lars Huang, and Sky Shi.

    Throughout the season, contestants visit five real-world locations, each tied to an exciting and meaningful themed story. In each story, every trainee is assigned a unique background and identity, belonging to different factions with distinct victory conditions. They compete for both personal and team honors through challenges that test their intelligence, observation, judgment, decision-making, communication, and teamwork skills.

  • Detective Academy S7

    • In "Detective Academy Season 7", the trainees include Eazin Pu, Stefan Guo, Cheese Qi, Vincent Cao, Yancy He, Jojo Tang, Lars Huang, Sky Shi, Dr. Ye (Huoshu), and Mark Li.

    The season is set against a narrative-driven backdrop, where contestants face six themed case assessments. Combining dynamic intellectual challenges with engaging game mechanics, the season showcases the positive and proactive spirit of the new generation, all while providing an entertaining and educational experience.

  • Detective Academy S8

    • In "Detective Academy Season 8", the trainees include Dr. Ye (Huoshu), Eazin Pu, Stefan Guo, Jacky Zhou, Cheese Qi, Rosalyn Luo, Vincent Cao, Yancy He, Mark Li, Jojo Tang, Mingming Shao, and Sky Shi.

    The season is centered around the theme "Team-building exploration and rule-based tasks." Multiple themed team-building scenarios are simulated throughout the season, with unconventional rules set in each scene. After interpreting and adapting to these rules, the contestants collaborate as a team to engage in logical reasoning and complete the assigned team-building challenges.
    Eazin Pu

    Eazin Pu (born April 20, 1994), male, Han ethnicity, from Mianyang, Sichuan Province, graduated with a major in Insurance from the School of Business at Nanjing University. He holds a master’s degree from Johns Hopkins University and works as a venture capitalist. He rose to fame in 2014 after participating in the show "The Brain". In 2015, he appeared on "New Youth of Cinema". In 2016, he competed on "Who’s Still Standing" and successfully defended his title for six consecutive episodes. In 2018, he received praise for his performance in "Who's the Murderer: Season 4". Since 2019, he has participated in "Great Escape: Expert Edition". In 2020, "Who's the Murderer: Detective College Season 2" aired. In 2021, he starred in the drama "My World Writes You". In 2022, he took part in "Super Brain Youth Team: Season 2".

    Biography

    Eazin Pu completed his undergraduate studies in Insurance at the Nanjing University School of Business. During college, he served as the captain of the popping dance team in the street dance club and is skilled at popping. He went abroad for postgraduate studies and obtained a Master’s degree in Finance from the Johns Hopkins University Business School. During high school, he won first prize in the National Mathematics Olympiad and second prize at the provincial level in the National High School Physics Competition. He enjoys watching movies and playing video games. His involvement in livestreaming is primarily driven by his desire to bring joy to others and promote high-quality games.

    Stefan Guo

    Stefan Guo (born April 10, 1994) was the top science student in the 2012 college entrance examination (Gaokao) in Qinghai Province, China. He holds a dual bachelor’s degree in Finance and Mathematics from the Guanghua School of Management at Peking University. He is a male singer, entertainer, and actor, and was formerly a finance professional. In 2017, he participated in the quiz show "Who’s Still Standing". Since 2019, he has been a regular participant in variety shows such as "Great Escape: Expert Edition", "Detective College", and "Who's the Murderer", serving as a core cast member of "Detective College".

    Biography and Career

    Guo Wentao scored 671 on the Gaokao, earning him the title of top science student in Qinghai Province in 2012. He was admitted to Peking University's Guanghua School of Management, where he majored in Finance and pursued a second degree in Mathematics. He once won the Qinghai Provincial Youth Sports Games’ table tennis team championship (boys group) and was runner-up in singles. During university, he participated in the production of several student microfilms. He has studied the Erhu (a traditional Chinese string instrument) since childhood and has achieved Level 8 proficiency. He also plays the guitar. During his appearance on "Who’s Still Standing", he performed the song "By the Baikal Lake" while playing the guitar.

    Jacky Zhou

    Jacky Zhou (born November 16, 1995) is a Chinese singer and actor. He graduated with a double major in Sociology and Psychology from McGill University in Canada and has worked as a research fellow at the Douglas College Institute of Psychology. He gained public attention in 2017 through his participation in "Speak to the World" and "I Am a Speaker: Season 4". In 2020, he played the role of Ruo Yu in the popular fantasy historical drama "Love and Redemption".

    Biography and Career

    Jacky Zhou graduated from McGill University in Canada with a double major in Sociology and Psychology. He later worked as a research fellow at the Douglas College Institute of Psychology. In music, he passed the Royal Conservatory of Music exams with Grade 8 in violin, First Class Honours in advanced music theory, and First Class Honours in beginner-level arranging. He also has experience with piano, guitar, and conducting.

    In sports, he was on his high school basketball team and won a city championship in Vancouver. He is also skilled in volleyball, baseball, mixed martial arts, and Savate (French boxing). During university, his team won the B Division championship in Montreal city competitions for two consecutive years.

    In debate, he was a debater and coach for his university’s Chinese-language debate team.

    Cheese Qi

    Cheese Qi, birth name Daize Qi, was born on April 13, 1994, in Tai’an, Shandong Province. He is a male TV host from mainland China, a national first-class announcer and presenter, and a graduate of the Broadcasting and Hosting program at the Communication University of China. In 2014, he hosted "Fresh Anchor’s View", a news commentary program on Yunnan Satellite TV. In 2015, he joined CCTV-1’s "Voice", a Chinese youth open lecture program, as a regular youth representative. In 2016, he signed with Mango Entertainment and became a TV host for Hunan TV. In February 2019, he began hosting "Sweet Mission", a Mango TV online celebrity interview variety show. On November 3, he became a regular guest on "Who's the Murderer: Detective College", a youth-oriented puzzle-solving reality show on Mango TV. On November 9, he competed in the "2019 CCTV National Host Competition" as a contestant in the arts category. In 2020, he hosted the voice actor competition show "The Sound Maker". Since 2021, he has hosted the panoramic music competition variety show "Call Me by Fire", as well as the female idol growth and inspiration show "Sisters Who Make Wave". Starting in 2022, he became the host of "The Great Detective: Tribunal", an internet-based legal education and reasoning show. In "2023", he hosted several major gala events, including "Xiaomang’s 2023 Courtesy Gal", Hunan TV & Mango TV Spring Festival Gala 2023, and “Spring Festival Gala for Overseas Chinese Worldwide”. On August 22, 2024, he was awarded "Host of the Year" at the 9th China Online Video Academy Awards. On August 30, he hosted the music competition show "Next Singer".

    Biography and Career

    Although Cheese Qi had been hosting various school events since he was young, it wasn't until his second year of high school that he learned—thanks to a teacher's recommendation—that "Broadcast Hosting" was an available major for the college entrance examination. During the summer break between his second and third year of high school, he met his first professional subject teacher, who introduced him to the name of the "Communication University of China" for the very first time. In October of his final year in high school, Qi followed his teacher to Beijing, where the Communication University of China was holding its annual "Qi Yue Festival"—the highest-level national competition in the field of recitation art, also known as the "National College Student Recitation Conference". That experience solidified his determination to apply to the Communication University of China. After setting this goal, Qi began practicing his vocal training every morning on the school sports field. During his first two years of university, Qi Sijun didn’t have a clear plan for his future career. His initial focus was simply to learn the professional skills from his instructors and to seize every opportunity to explore and gain experience. While interning, he worked in various roles related to program production—such as on-camera reporter, program director, and videographer. These experiences proved to be extremely valuable. By the time he reached his senior year, Qi received job offers from multiple media platforms during the interview season.

    Jojo Tang

    Jojo Tang (born February 5, 1998) is a male singer from Changchun, Jilin Province, China. He graduated with a degree in Industrial Design from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. In 2018 he made his debut by participating as a member of the trainee host group on Mango TV’s variety show "Hey, Kids!" ("Gege Bie Nao La"). In addition to hosting, he later appeared in a variety of popular shows, including "Great Escape: Expert Edition", "Who's the Murderer", and "Detective College". In 2021, he competed as a trainee under Yihua Entertainment on iQIYI’s idol survival show "Youth With You Season 3" and its related spin-off programs. On July 25, 2021, he officially debuted as a member of the limited boy group "IXFORM".

    Biography and Career

    Jojo Tang was born on February 5, 1998, in Changchun, Jilin Province. A strong student academically, he scored 585.5 points on the 2013 high school entrance exam and was admitted to the Affiliated High School of Northeast Normal University. In 2016, he scored 647 points on the national college entrance examination (Gaokao)—642 raw score plus 5 bonus points. With a talent for mathematics and a passion for art, he chose to pursue Industrial Design at the School of Digital Media and Design Arts at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, a program that combines both disciplines.During university, Tang served as president of the Volunteer Association and was also a member of the university’s Communist Youth League committee. Impressively, he completed all required credits by his third year.

    Mingming Shao

    Mingming Shao, birth name Mingzhu Shao, was born on October 25, 1997 in Shanghai with ancestral roots in Suzhou, Anhui Province. He is a TV host, singer, and actor from mainland China. Shao graduated from the Department of Music and Performing Arts at Mianyang Teachers’ College.

    Early Life

    Mingming Shao was born in Shanghai and showed a love for singing and dancing from a young age. In middle school he began learning the guzheng (a traditional Chinese string instrument), and by his first year of high school， he started studying vocal music under a professional instructor. For the college entrance examination he was admitted to the Music and Performing Arts program at Mianyang Teachers' College in Sichuan， performing a Level 10 guzheng piece， the highest level in the grading system. During his university years, he actively participated in a variety of school events, including anniversary galas, hosting competitions, and speech contests. He earned several awards, such as:

    • Outstanding Individual in the "Youth Blooms in the Military Camp" National Day Performance
    • Best Program Award at the 33rd Mianyang Chrysanthemum Festival Opening Ceremony
    • First Prize in the “Academy Cup” University Art Festival Hosting Competition at Mianyang Teachers' College>
    • Third Prize in the Instrumental Music Competition

    Vincent Cao

    Vincent Cao, born Sida Cao on November 26, 1995, in Beijing, China, is a mainland Chinese film and television actor. He graduated from the Piano Department of the Central Conservatory of Music.

    Early Life

    In 2013, Cao won first place at the Hong Kong International Piano Competition. He officially entered the entertainment industry in 2017, debuting in the film "Mom, I’m Your Daughter"(Wo Shi Ni Ma). That same year, he played Chen An in the youth campus web drama "My Huckleberry Friends"(Ni Hao, Jiu Shi Guang). In 2020, he starred in the youth inspirational drama "Twenty Your Life On" (Er Shi Bu Huo) and the suspense series "A Murderous Affair in Horizon Tower" (Mo Tian Da Lou). In November 2020, he joined the cast of the mystery and logic game variety show "Who's the Murderer: Detective College Season 3" as a trainee and became a regular guest.

      From 2021 to 2022, he appeared in several dramas including:
    • Dear Missy (Liao Bu Qi De Nu Hai),
    • Life Is a Game (Fan Zhuan Ren Sheng),
    • School Is Starting, Bo Ren Boys! (Kai Xue Ba, Bo Ren Shao Nian),

    In 2023, he starred in the modern Chinese-style romance drama "Crush on You" (Hen Xiang Hen Xiang Ni), for which he won the Most Challenging Actor Award at the 4th New Era International TV Festival. In 2024, he is set to play the lead role in the suspense film "Everyone’s a Suspect" (Quan Yuan Xian Yi Ren).

    Yancy He

    Yancy He, born on October 21, 1994, in Zhejiang Province, is a lawyer and reality TV personality from mainland China. He earned his bachelor’s degree from Zhejiang University of Technology and later completed his master’s degree at Beihang University (Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics).

    Early Life

    In 2019, He gained public attention by participating in the first season of the legal reality show "Heartwarming Offer" (Ling Ren Xin Dong de Offer), where he successfully received an offer to begin his legal career. On March 1, 2019, he obtained his legal practice qualification. From 2021 to 2024, Yancy He became a recurring guest in various popular variety shows, including:

    • Who's the Murderer series (Ming Xing Da Zhen Tan),
    • Detective College series (Ming Zhen Tan Xue Yuan),
    • Heartwarming Offer Season 5,
    • A Journey Into Geography Books (Tiao Jin Di Li Shu de Lü Xing)
    On August 18, 2022, he began his full-time legal practice at AllBright Law Offices (Shanghai). On November 27, 2023, he took part in the 2nd "Corporate Law Youth Talk" academic forum held at the University of International Business and Economics. As of November 28, 2023, he is practicing law at Grandall Law Firm (Shanghai) [国枫律师事务所].

    Sky Shi

    Sky Shi (born December 14, 1999) is a Chinese baritone singer and a graduate of the Vocal Music Department of Sichuan Normal University. In 2018, he participated in the first season of Hunan TV’s bel canto music show "Super–Vocal", where he was the youngest performer of the season. In 2019, he joined Mango TV’s variety program "The Detectives’ Adventures" (Ming Zhen Tan Xue Yuan), once again becoming the youngest cast member on the show.

    Biography and Career

    In November 2018, Sky Shi made his official debut as a performing member on Hunan TV’s vocal performance show "Super–Vocal Season 1". In March 2019, he released his first digital single, "The Elk King", marking a step outside his classical bel canto style with a bright and upbeat melody that resonated with youthful energy. In November 2019, he joined the reality game show "The Detectives’ Adventures: Season 1" as an “academy trainee,” later becoming a regular cast member. On June 3, 2020, he co-wrote and performed the show’s theme song, "NO.1 — Nan Bo Wan". On July 29, he participated in the real-life escape room reality show "Great Escape: Elite Edition Season 2", and eventually became a regular on the program. In 2022, he released a series of singles including "Exclusive Listener", "YOU", "Finale Performance", and "Not Tomorrow, But Now". In 2023, he followed up with tracks like "Thanks", "Blank", "Forever Passionate", and "Romance", and launched his “Project K” solo concert tour in September. In April 2024, he released his first physical album, "Counting Stars All Night". In June, he kicked off his second solo tour, titled "Blooming Along the Way". In August, he participated in the fourth season of the music competition show "Call Me by Fire". In September, he received the “Weibo Rising Musician” award at the 2024 Weibo Music Awards. In December, he appeared in the drama series "Thanks, Class Three".
